    2022 NFL Season Outlook

    A project by Scott Weitman and Jonah Paulino

    About our Project

    As student football managers, our view on football is different from others. Having an inside look into what goes on in the day-to-day operations of a football team allow us to see what coaches stress and what they do not. Because there were aspects that were stressed and others that were not, we began to inquire if some statistics in football led to more success than others. In order to test this, we looked at data from the 2022 NFL season to see if certain statistics were more indicative than others. Just a few clicks away is our findings.
